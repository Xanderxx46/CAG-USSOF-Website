Operator Roles

Name Description MOS Code
Combat Engineer As a Combat Engineer you will be in charge of building trenches to keep our troops safe. You will also be in charge of Close Quaters Combat (CQB) and breaching buildings if nessasary for the mission. 12B
Demolitions Expert As a Demolitions Expert you will be in charge of laying explosives on buildings and other important targets we must destroy. You will also be in charge of detecting mines so our troops can avoid them. 18C
Sniper/Marksman As a Marksman, you will be in charge of taking out targets from long distances, as well as ensuring those targets are dead. You will also be in charge of keeping an eye on our troops from a distance to make sure they are still alive. 18B
Combat Medic As a Combat Medic you will be the main one keeping our troops in fighting condition. You will be in charge of ensuring they are stitched up and not dying on the ground. 18D
Grenadier As a Grenadier you will use a grenade launcher to eliminate groups of targets from a distance. You will use the weapon to ensure our forces are not overwhelmed. 11B
Rifleman As a Rifleman you will be carrying a regular rifle, but you're just as important as anyone. You will likely be carrying ammo for the Machine Gunners/Marksman, or the carrying explosives for the Combat Engineer/Demolitions Expert 11B
Machine Gunner As a Machine Gunner, you will be the one to suppress targets. You will use your machine gun and some skillful tactics to ensure the targets are too scared to peek their head up and shoot at us. 18B
Joint Terminal Attack Controller (JTAC) As a JTAC, you will be the main one talking to command and other far away entities. You will be the one with the knowledge to skillfully talk to people far away with precision and motive. You will also be in charge of ensuring the whole squad knows the important info. 13F

Unit Biography

Semi-milsim unit primarily focused on special operations and small team tactics. We like to keep things structured, but we always prioritize keeping things fun while being organized. Our missions are designed to challenge teamwork and precision. We are a closely knitted community and we love tactical realism. Our goal is to create an enjoyable and immersive experience for all.
